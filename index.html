OUR STORY

EdWisor was founded in 2015 to empower over 100 million students and professionals across the nation. The efforts were inclined towards providing an end to end solution to the skill and employability problem as per industry demands. With a vision to transform 1 million lives by 2021, we have already positively impacted 5000+ students along with 200+ companies. Our Vision is to empower students & professionals with skills & opportunities in high growth careers of their choice. edWisor aims to bring a positive change in the careers of at least 1 million professionals each year starting from the year 2021, and that is what motivates us to come & work for students every day.
